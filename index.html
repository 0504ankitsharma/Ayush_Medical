Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common queries about Arogya Mitra and our healthcare innovations. If you have more questions, don't hesitate to get in touch. Your well-being matters to us, and we're here to help.

  • How does the QR Code drug ATM work?

    The QR Code drug ATM is a cutting-edge solution that simplifies medication dispensing. When you receive a prescription, it includes a QR code. You can scan this QR code at our drug ATM, which will then dispense the prescribed medication. This technology eliminates the need to wait in long pharmacy queues, providing quick and efficient access to your medications.

  • Is my data secure when using the QR Code drug ATM?

    Yes, your data security is a top priority for us. We have implemented stringent measures to protect your prescription information and ensure privacy. Our system complies with data protection laws and employs encryption to safeguard your data throughout the process.

  • What if I have trouble scanning the QR code on my prescription?

    If you encounter difficulties scanning the QR code, our onsite staff is ready to assist you. They can manually enter the prescription details to ensure you receive your medication promptly.

  • Can I use the QR Code Drug ATM for all types of medications?

    The QR Code Drug ATM is designed to dispense a wide range of medications, including Medicines. However, for certain specialized medications or controlled substances, you may still need to visit a pharmacy counter.

  • Do I need any special app or device to use the QR Code Drug ATM?

    No, you don't need any additional app or device. The QR Code Drug ATM is designed to be user-friendly. Simply use the camera on your smartphone or tablet to scan the QR code on your prescription, and the ATM will handle the rest.
